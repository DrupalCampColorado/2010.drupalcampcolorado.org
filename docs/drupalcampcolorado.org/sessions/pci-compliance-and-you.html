PCI Compliance and YOU!

Posted by m_rookie on May 5th, 2010

If you ever deal with credit cards these new standards are going to effect you.

PCI Compliance must be obtained by any retailer or developer that charges or store credit card data.

During this session we will go over basic do's and don'ts for PCI Compliance, using our own compliance project as a case study.
