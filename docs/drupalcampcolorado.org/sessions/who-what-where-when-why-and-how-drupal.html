The Who, What, Where, When, Why and How of Drupal

Posted by skessler on May 12th, 2010

Are you struggling to wrap your head around Drupal? Then this session is for you. We will start at the very beginning with the who, what, where, when, why and how of Drupal and wind our way through this amazing system. This session is designed for those first learning about Drupal, IT professionals looking to vet Drupal for their organizations, and non-technical users looking to understand how their use case fits into the Drupal eco-system. In this session we will focus on what is currently available in Drupal 6 but also look at key changes in Drupal 7 that are relevant to understanding the platform. We will make sure to take lots of questions from the audience for this session so start writing down all your burning Drupal questions.
