Pixel Apropos

Pixel Apropos is a small development shop focusing on content presentation, whether that means building the view, theming the presentation, or copyediting the content. We can turn a beautiful idea into a functional website, or we can take a technically functioning website and make it friendlier with appropriate graphics and layout.

In our subcontract work, we have created custom themes for larger Drupal installations and simple layouts for small hand-coded sites. We also develop specific solutions, such as printing a page or node type as desired or javascripting an interactive function. In addition to our work for development companies, we also do consulting work for businesses and nonprofits, finding and implementing ways to improve their sites or custom building sites from scratch.
