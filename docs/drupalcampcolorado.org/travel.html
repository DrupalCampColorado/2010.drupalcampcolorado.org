Travel

DrupalCamp Colorado 2010 will be held on the Auraria Higher Education Center near downtown Denver.

Location

Sessions will be held in the North Classroom of the Auraria Higher Education Center.


View DrupalCamp CO 2010 in a larger map

AHEC Campus and parking map: http://www.ahec.edu/parking/ADAparking.pdf

Light rail, bus, etc.

The location is convenient for many modes of mass transit with both bus and light rail stations within a few blocks. You can use the above google map to get directions from your home, then select "bus" mode to see options and schedule. RTD Denver has more details on public transport.

Biking

Ride your bike! The platte and Cherry Creek trails will both get you right near the campus from miles around.

Parking

Recommended visitor parking is in the Tivoli Lot at Ninth St and Auraria Parkway located directly west of the Tivoli Student Union. The rate is $1.50 per half hour with a maximum for the day of $10. Use this PDF and map to see where the parking structure relates to the North Building where the conference is held.

Lodging

Being so close to downtown Denver there are many nearby hotels.

The Hilton Garden Inn, Downtown Denver is offering a special rate of $145/night for all DCCO attendees. There is a limited number of rooms available and reservations must be made by Mon. June 16th in order to take advantage of this offer. The hotel has in-room internet access, microwaves, refrigerators and is walking distance to both the camp venue and 16th St. Mall.

To make reservations contact the hotel directly and tell them DrupalCamp Colorado sent you.
