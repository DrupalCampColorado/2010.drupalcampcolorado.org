Interview with Page1Solutions

Posted by juaneclark on June 21st, 2010

What does your shop do and how do you work with Drupal?

Page1Solutions is a web marketing firm for the legal and medical fields. We provide a range of services for our clients including: fully custom website design and development, custom video production, search engine marketing utilizing comprehensive optimization, pay per click, online public relations, and more. Drupal has proven to be a versatile tool, for both content management and custom development, helping us meet two of our biggest client demands.

How did you first get involved in Drupal development?

We first got involved in Drupal development about two years ago. At the time, we evaluated a number of content management systems and decided to go with Drupal for a variety reasons: our commitment to open source, the active community behind Drupal, and the scalability and flexibility of the Drupal framework.

What type of work and/or websites do you specialize in?

Page1Solutions specializes in web marketing for clients in the cosmetic dentistry, plastic surgery, bariatric surgery, LASIK and ophthalmology, and legal fields. Our sites are primarily brochure websites aimed at marketing our client's practice and educating users in our syndications.

What are you most looking forward to at DrupalCamp Colorado?

Connecting with the local Drupal community and learning new (and often better) ways to develop with Drupal are what we're looking forward to the most. Supporting and engaging with the Drupal community is one of the primary reasons we decided to sponsor!

Is there anything else you'd like our attendees to know?

Just that we're very excited to join the Drupal community and have the opportunity to help sponsor DrupalCamp!
