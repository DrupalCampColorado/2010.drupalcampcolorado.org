Session schedule

Posted by Ben on June 14th, 2010

We've selected the sessions, with the help of your vote, which will be presented at DrupalCamp Colorado in Denver this June 26 & 27th. You can see the schedule at http://drupalcampcolorado.org/schedule.

Schedule

Saturday begins at 8:45am for registration checkin and ends a bit after 6pm. Lunch is 1.5 hours both days. Thirty six sessions have been selected for this Camp! We'll also have some social parties planned around the event, more on that later this week. Check it out at http://drupalcampcolorado.org/schedule.

BOFs

If your session wasn't selected or you'd like to focus in on some other topics you should suggest an informal gathering known as "birds of a feather" or BOFs. These gatherings, community organized during or between the selected sessions, are often of great value because the groups are smaller and are carried out in discussion style, rather than presenter and attendee.

Organize BOFs by posting to the forum http://drupalcampcolorado.org/forums/drupalcamp-colorado-forum with the topic and time.

Travel

The downtown Hilton Garden Inn is offering a discount rate on rooms for the next couple days so act fast if you haven't secured lodging. More at http://drupalcampcolorado.org/travel. We'll also have some details on parking at or near the venue soon.

DrupalCamp Colorado is less than two weeks away! If you're presenting be sure to get your deck and talk prepared and pass the word about this conference on to your colleagues and friends. Registration price increases next week to $50.

Post to your website or blog that you're attending DrupalCamp Colorado http://drupalcampcolorado.org/content/badges!
